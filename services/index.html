Services

Full-spectrum support from before you arrive to after you settle in.

Every stage of a student's journey has specific challenges. We've mapped them all and built support around each one.

Our Services

1.0

Pre-Arrival

Immigration readiness review, document checklists, and a complete arrival compliance pack so nothing gets missed before departure.

2.0

Housing

Needs assessment, verified housing options, video walkthroughs of units, lease review by our team, and full move-in coordination.

3.0

Arrival & First 72 Hours

Airport pickup, SIM card activation, escort to residence, welcome kit with essentials, and a city orientation to get students grounded fast.

4.0

Life Skills & Independence

A Mandarin-speaking mentor helps with budgeting, transit orientation, grocery shopping, neighborhood amenities, and everyday confidence.

5.0

Health & Wellness

Family physician setup, mental health resource connections, insurance navigation, and ongoing wellness check-ins.

6.0

Cultural & Emotional Support

Same-language mentors, regular check-ins, social events with other international students, and cultural norms briefings to ease the transition.

7.0

Emergency Support

24/7 access to a real person, emergency hotline, hospital and accident coordination, and immediate family liaison when it matters most.

8.0

Academic Transition

Course enrollment guidance, timetable planning support, and connections to academic resources at the student's institution.

Packages

Two tiers of support, both comprehensive.

Every Brightway family gets real, ongoing support. The tier you choose determines how deep and how often.

Premium

$4,680/year

  • Pre-arrival preparation
  • Airport pickup & first 72-hour support
  • Verified housing assistance
  • Monthly check-ins & parent updates
  • 24/7 emergency line
  • Life skills mentorship

Ultimate

$7,680/year

  • Everything in Premium
  • Dedicated case manager
  • Weekly check-ins & bi-weekly parent updates
  • Full housing coordination & lease review
  • Health & wellness setup
  • Academic transition support
  • Cultural mentorship & social integration
