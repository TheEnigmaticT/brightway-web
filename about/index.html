About Us

The trusted companion for students studying away from home.

Brightway is a concierge guardianship service built for international families navigating life in Canada.

Our Mission

Ensuring safety, comfort, and personal growth through culturally-aware support.

International students deserve more than paperwork guardianship. They need someone who understands the cultural context, speaks their language, and shows up when it matters. Brightway exists to bridge the gap between families at home and students finding their footing abroad.

Our Story

Built from a simple observation: most guardianship services are just signatures on paper.

Brightway was founded on the belief that international students need real, ongoing, human support, not a name on a form. We built a concierge guardianship model that combines local knowledge, cultural fluency, and proactive care to help students thrive and give families peace of mind.

Our Team

Local teams across three Canadian cities.

Every city where we operate has a dedicated team of advisors, housing specialists, and multilingual mentors who know the local systems inside out.

Vancouver

Our founding team. Deep connections with universities across Metro Vancouver, housing networks, and healthcare providers who work with international students.

Toronto

Canada's largest student city. Our Toronto team covers the GTA's sprawling campus ecosystem and knows every transit route, clinic, and rental district.

Montreal

A bilingual city with unique systems. Our Montreal team navigates French-language requirements, RAMQ health coverage, and the city's distinct housing market.
