Partnerships

Your students land in Canada. We make sure they land on their feet.

Brightway partners with institutions, recruitment agents, and organizations to deliver the on-the-ground support that turns placements into success stories.

For Institutions & Universities

Stop losing international students to preventable problems.

Retention starts with support. Brightway works directly with universities and colleges to provide guardianship, housing, wellness, and crisis response so your international students stay enrolled, engaged, and thriving.

Orientation & wellness programs

We run structured arrival support that complements your existing orientation, covering the gaps institutional programs miss: housing reality, city navigation, healthcare setup.

Housing & host-family support

Verified housing, in-person inspections, and move-in coordination. No student shows up to find a unit that doesn't match the listing.

Safeguarding & compliance

Full legal guardianship, parent communication protocols, and crisis management that meets duty-of-care standards.

For Recruitment Agents

Your reputation depends on what happens after admission.

Families remember the agent who placed their child. When things go wrong in Canada, they call you. Brightway ensures those calls are good news, not crises.

Higher retention, fewer complaints

Students who feel supported stay enrolled. Families who receive regular updates stay satisfied. Your placement success rate goes up.

Transparent reporting

Structured updates flow to both families and your agency. You're never caught off guard when a parent asks how their child is doing.

Revenue-share partnership

Clear terms, structured tiers, and a partnership model that rewards the students you refer with genuine, ongoing care.

What Partners Get

A complete post-arrival support layer for every student you send.

Legal guardianship

Full custodianship plus real, ongoing involvement. Not a signature on a form.

Housing coordination

Verified housing, in-person inspections, lease review, and move-in support.

Arrival & orientation

Airport pickup, SIM activation, city orientation, and first-week essentials.

24/7 emergency response

A real person answers. Hospital coordination, accident response, immediate family contact.

Ongoing wellness

Regular check-ins, mental health resources, and proactive issue detection.

Structured reporting

Monthly updates to families and partner organizations on student wellbeing and progress.

How It Works

Three steps from referral to ongoing support.

01

Referral

Your organization refers a student or family. We receive their profile, program details, and specific needs.

02

Onboarding

Family onboarding call, personalized support plan, pre-arrival preparation, and housing coordination begin immediately.

03

Continuous care

From airport pickup through the full academic year: check-ins, crisis support, parent updates, and regular reports back to your organization.
