Partnerships

Building Global Partnerships to Empower International Students

Crestway Global collaborates with institutions, recruitment agents, and businesses worldwide to create seamless support, opportunities, and growth for students.

For Institutions & Universities

Bridge the gap between admission and successful settlement.

Crestway Global does not recruit students and does not interfere with academic decision-making. We exist to make your international students more successful after they arrive.

Fewer non-academic escalations

Housing crises, health emergencies, and homesickness rarely reach your international office. We handle them before they become your problem.

Protected students, reassured parents

Families feel confident their child has local support. That confidence translates to fewer worried calls to your admissions team.

Reduced administrative workload

Your admissions, international offices, and faculty can focus on academics while we handle settlement logistics and day-to-day student support.

For Recruitment Agents

Your trusted partner in Canada for post-admission support.

We complement, not replace, your core recruitment role. When families remember who helped their child succeed in Canada, your name comes up.

Students who transition successfully

Your placements don't just arrive—they thrive. Students who feel supported stay enrolled and recommend you to others.

Stronger parent trust

When you recommend Crestway Global alongside placement, parents see you as someone who cares about outcomes, not just commissions.

Fewer post-arrival complaints

No more 3am calls about housing disasters or health scares. Emergencies get handled locally, and you stay informed without being overwhelmed.

Higher retention and satisfaction

Supported students complete their programs. Satisfied families send siblings and cousins. Your referral network grows.

For Businesses & Organizations

Access motivated, global student talent.

Crestway Global partners with local and global businesses to offer internships, volunteer programs, and experiential learning opportunities for international students.

Global talent pipeline

Connect with motivated international students who bring diverse perspectives, multilingual skills, and fresh energy to your team.

Positive brand engagement

Associate your organization with international education and cross-cultural development. Students remember companies that gave them their first Canadian opportunity.

Trusted Canadian partnership

Work with an established support service that vets students and ensures they're prepared, reliable, and eager to contribute.

Why Crestway Global

The partner your students deserve.

Trusted expertise

A highly experienced team bringing deep knowledge and hands-on experience across diverse roles within the international education sector.

Student-centric approach

We prioritize student safety, well-being, and success. When students thrive, everyone wins.

Comprehensive support

Guardianship, academic support, housing, wellness, crisis management, and more—all coordinated through a single trusted partner.

Seamless collaboration

Transparent processes, clear structured reporting, and partnership-focused solutions. You always know how your referred students are doing.

Global perspective

We connect institutions, agents, and students worldwide. Our team understands both Canadian systems and the cultures our students come from.

Let's build something that works for your students.

Whether you're a university, recruitment agent, or business organization, we'd like to show you what structured, on-the-ground support looks like in practice.
