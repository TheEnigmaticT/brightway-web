Common Concerns

Questions Chinese parents ask us

Will my child be safe in Canada?

Canada is among the safest countries for international students. We add an extra layer: verified housing, known contacts in emergencies, and advisors who check in regularly. We treat your child's safety as if they were our own.

How do I know what's really happening if I'm so far away?

Our monthly reports cover academics, health, social life, and emotional wellbeing. We include specifics: grades, doctor visits, activities, mood. If something is wrong, we tell you immediately. No surprises.

My child is very independent. Will they feel watched?

Our support is available, not imposed. Independent students use us as a resource when needed. We respect their autonomy while remaining available for guidance.

What if my child gets sick?

We help them register with a family doctor, understand their insurance, and navigate the healthcare system. For serious issues, we accompany them to appointments and keep you informed every step of the way.
