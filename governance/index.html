Governance & Safeguarding

Accountability built into every process.

Professional standards, documented procedures, and transparent oversight for families and partner institutions.

Safeguarding

Student protection protocols

Staff Vetting

  • Comprehensive background checks for all advisors
  • Vulnerable sector screening where required
  • Reference verification and employment history review
  • Ongoing performance monitoring

Communication Standards

  • All student interactions documented in our system
  • Response time standards: 4 hours for routine, 1 hour for urgent
  • Clear escalation pathways for serious concerns
  • Regular supervision of advisor-student relationships

Boundary Maintenance

  • Clear policies on appropriate advisor conduct
  • Professional relationship guidelines
  • Reporting mechanisms for boundary concerns
  • Annual ethics training for all staff

Emergency Protocols

  • Documented procedures for medical emergencies
  • Mental health crisis response guidelines
  • Family notification procedures
  • Coordination with university and local authorities

Privacy & Data

Information handling and family rights

Data Protection

  • Compliance with Canadian privacy legislation (PIPEDA)
  • Encrypted storage for all student and family information
  • Limited access: only assigned advisors see case details
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

Family Access

  • Parents retain right to request information about their child
  • Regular update reports provided monthly
  • Direct advisor contact for questions and concerns
  • Clear policies on what information can be shared

Third-Party Sharing

  • Information shared with universities only when necessary
  • Healthcare providers contacted with student/family consent
  • No data sold or shared for commercial purposes
  • Partners bound by confidentiality agreements

Insurance & Liability

Coverage and professional accountability

Professional Liability

  • Comprehensive errors and omissions insurance
  • Coverage for advisory and coordination services
  • Clear scope of service limitations documented

Student Coverage

  • Students required to maintain health insurance
  • Guidance provided on Canadian healthcare enrollment
  • Coordination with insurance providers for claims

Reporting & Transparency

How we maintain accountability

Incident Documentation

  • All significant incidents recorded within 24 hours
  • Families notified of relevant incidents promptly
  • Root cause analysis for systemic issues
  • Process improvements implemented based on lessons learned

Family Feedback

  • Quarterly satisfaction surveys
  • Open channel for concerns and suggestions
  • Response to feedback within 48 hours
  • Annual review of service standards

University Partnership

  • Regular coordination meetings with partner institutions
  • Clear protocols for information sharing
  • Respect for university policies and procedures
  • Joint problem-solving for student welfare issues
